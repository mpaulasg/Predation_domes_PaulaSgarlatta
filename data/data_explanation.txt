data_predation_squidpops:

attack per video: the amount of violents attacks per video
attack per hour: number of attacks in one hour
attacking_minutes: sum of minutes that the predator is attacking the dome
pursue per video: the amount of approachings per video (and/or touching the dome)
pursue per hour: number of approaching in one hour
pursue_minutes: sum of minutes that the predator is approaching the dome
circling per video: the amount of circling per video 
circling per hour: number of circling in one hour
circling_minutes: sum of minutes that the predator is circling

For squidpops we only have baits, so only number of attacks per hour will be used.
